## About TaskHunt

TaskHunt connects users and verified local taskers to complete daily tasks such as
rides, shopping, deliveries, and small errands.

It is designed for students, professionals, and gig workers who want
flexible earning opportunities in India.

TaskHunt works as a community-based alternative to platforms like
Dunzo, Rapido, Urban Company, Swiggy Genie, and Porter.
